About Us

Our project aims to develop a comprehensive banking application using Django, offering a range of features to facilitate seamless financial transactions and user management. The application encompasses functionalities such as user authentication, online Know Your Customer (KYC) registration, fund transfers, payment requests, transaction history tracking, and management of credit/debit cards.

CEO - Dr. Usama Mir

Our Key Features

Our features, are what making us different.

User Authentication

The application provides robust user authentication mechanisms to ensure secure access to the banking services.

Online KYC Registration

Our platform streamlines the KYC process, allowing users to complete their Know Your Customer verification online.

Fund Transfer

Users can transfer funds between their own accounts or to other registered accounts within the platform.

Payment Request

The application facilitates payment requests, enabling users to request payments from other account holders.

Transaction History

Users have access to their transaction history, allowing them to view and track all past financial transactions.

Card Management

Users can add, update, or remove credit/debit cards associated with their accounts.
